PathCase is an academic research tool, and we would be more
than happy to share our knowledge with other researchers who
could benefit from it.

 * The PathCase source and database are provided freely for
    academic use.
 * If you are interested in using PathCase for commercial
    purposes, please contact us at pathcase@eecs.case.edu
    with your name, organization, and what you would like to
    use PathCase for.

Pathway visualization requires a layout generation license
from Tom Sawyer Software (http://www.tomsawyer.com).  Please
contact Tom Sawyer Software directly to obtain the required
license.
